# Privacy Policy for init.habits

Effective date: May 19, 2026

init.habits is a productivity and habit tracking app that helps users build routines, track habits, review progress, and stay consistent.

## Information We Collect

init.habits may collect or process the following information:

- Email address and user account identifiers when you sign in with email/password or Google Sign-In.
- Basic profile information provided by Google Sign-In, such as name or profile identifier, when made available by Google.
- App activity such as screen views, feature usage, habit creation, habit completion, and other app interaction events through Firebase Analytics.
- Device or other identifiers used by Firebase and Google services for authentication, analytics, diagnostics, fraud prevention, and app reliability.

Habit names, completion history, journal entries, reminders, settings, and progress data are stored locally on your device unless a future sync feature is added.

## How We Use Information

We use information for:

- Providing account sign-in and authentication.
- Operating core app features.
- Understanding app usage and improving the product.
- Monitoring reliability and diagnosing issues.
- Protecting the app from misuse or unauthorized access.

We do not sell personal data. We do not show third-party ads.

## Third-Party Services

init.habits uses Firebase and Google services, including Firebase Authentication, Google Sign-In, and Firebase Analytics. These services may process data according to Google's privacy policies and service terms.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase privacy and security information:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with advertisers or data brokers. Data may be processed by Google/Firebase as service providers for authentication, analytics, diagnostics, and app functionality.

## Data Security

Data transmitted to Firebase and Google services is encrypted in transit. Local habit data remains on your device unless you choose to export, back up, or share it.

## Account And Data Deletion

You may request account or data deletion by contacting the project maintainer through the GitHub Issues page:

https://github.com/lekhanpro/init-habits/issues

If you contact us, include the email address associated with your account so we can identify the relevant account data.

## Children's Privacy

init.habits is not designed for children. The app is intended for a general productivity audience and is not directed to users under 13.

## Health Disclaimer

init.habits is a productivity and routine tracking app. It is not a medical or healthcare app and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. Updates will be posted in this file with a new effective date.
